European Venture Retreat

A community of 60 entrepreneurs and investors surrounded by the Dolomites

WHEN

September 14-17, 2017

Apply to attend

A weekend to unplug and connect.

HOW WAS IT?

Another conference?

This is not a conference. It is a weekend in the mountains, with serious hiking and bunk bed sleeping.

It is an occasion to really deeply connect with fellow entrepreneurs and investors, but most of all to feel the pull of nature, enjoying the silence and solitude that you can only get by being in the middle of the mountains.

We will spend 3 nights in the beautiful Dolomites, sleeping at 2,400m high up where you can only hear your own breath.

The event is deliberately light in its schedule, to maximize the amount of time you can spend actually getting to know the other participants, as well as meditating with yourself.
